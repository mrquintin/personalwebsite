15/P02 — BIO BLOCK
====================

GOAL
----
A short bio block under the hero. 2-4 sentences. Tells the visitor
what the user does and what perspective they bring. No life story.

§A FILES TOUCHED
----------------
  src/components/landing/Bio.tsx         (NEW)
  src/content/landing/bio.ts             (NEW — bio copy)

§B ACCEPTANCE
-------------
B1.  src/content/landing/bio.ts exports:
       export const bio = {
         heading: "Briefly",     // mono small heading
         body: [
           "<sentence 1>",
           "<sentence 2>",
           "<optional sentence 3>",
           "<optional sentence 4>",
         ],
       };
     Body sentences are sourced from src/content/about/biography.ts
     and identity.ts. The implementer composes a v2 bio from
     existing fragments rather than authoring new prose. If the
     existing bio is too long (more than 4 sentences fit), pick the
     four most informative and note in a comment which were dropped.

B2.  Bio.tsx layout:
       <Surface tone="mute" padding={6} border>
         <Stack gap=3>
           <p className="t-meta" style={{ color: var(--accent) }}>
             {heading}
           </p>
           {body.map(s => <p className="t-prose">{s}</p>)}
         </Stack>
       </Surface>

B3.  Container size="base" wraps the section. Vertical padding:
     var(--s-6) above and below.

B4.  No images, no avatar.

§C VERIFICATION COMMANDS
------------------------
  test -f src/components/landing/Bio.tsx
  test -f src/content/landing/bio.ts

§D NON-GOALS
------------
  • Do NOT extend the bio beyond 4 sentences.
  • Do NOT include credentials/awards in the landing bio. /resume covers them.
