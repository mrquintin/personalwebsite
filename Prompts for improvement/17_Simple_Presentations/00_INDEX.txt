PROMPT SET 17 — SIMPLE PRESENTATIONS
======================================

CONTEXT
-------
The user's directive: "I love the little presentations. I think
that they should be much simpler, though, and much more focused on
just explaining what the products are and what they do, at a
relatively simple level, like, not going too deep into them."

v2 presentations are 3-5 phases of pure prose, click-through, no
inline widgets. Embedded inside each project page (not standalone).

DIFFERENCE FROM PRIOR SUITE 11 (archived)
-----------------------------------------
Suite 11 had 7-8 phase decks with pedagogical widgets per phase
(quadrants, diamonds, sliders). v2 drops the widgets entirely. Per
principle 4 (words over widgets): a paragraph that conveys the same
point is preferred to a widget. The chassis stays small.
