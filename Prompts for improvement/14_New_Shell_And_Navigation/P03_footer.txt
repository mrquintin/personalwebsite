         external: [
           { label: "GitHub", href: "https://github.com/<user>" },
           { label: "Email",  href: "mailto:..." },
           { label: "LinkedIn", href: "https://linkedin.com/in/..." },

B2.  Layout: full-width strip at the bottom of every page. Border-top

     desktop:

             var(--t-sm-size).
       Right: cluster of links from footerLinks.external (and .site
              if non-empty), separated by " · " in --fg-faint.
     Single-column on mobile, stacked.

B3.  External links use Link variant="subtle" external=true (so they
     open in new tabs and get the ↗ glyph from suite 13/P05).

B4.  No animations. No newsletter signup. No social icons. Plain text.
